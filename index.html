About

The Archery Club is a sport club for Ohio State University students, faculty and alumni with an interest in the sport of archery. Many of our members joined as first-time shooters who have grown into skilled archers with the help of our nationally ranked coaches and their unwavering commitment and support.

We believe in encouraging and supporting anyone who wants to learn to shoot by providing equipment and various practice opportunities to enhance the learning experience. Practice

Who?

You, of course!

No experience is needed to join the club. We have experienced members who are able to teach you all of the basics you need to shoot your first arrow and guide you through proper form and technique so that you are one step closer to a perfect shot.

Where?

Our practice space is located in the arena at Plumb Hall on West Campus.

The building is easily accessible by the NE, CLN, CLS, and BV CABS buses, the University Village bus and COTA lines #18 and #84. Paid and permit parking is also available in the parking lot beside Plumb.

When?

Practice times are from 6-8pm every Monday - Friday except during Winter, Spring, Summer and Fall Breaks.

Should practice ever be canceled for another reason, members will be notified by email and through our Twitter.

Do Sports Easy

All new and returning members are required by RecSports to fill out a Do Sports Easy every year before any equipment can be touched.

Safety

Equipment

We provide all of the necessary equipment you need to be an archer. This includes:

  • Armguards
  • Finger Tabs
  • Recurve Bows
  • Arrows
  • Ground Quivers

If you have your own equipment, it is highly recommended that you bring it. Limited onsite storage space is available; please approach one of our officers for more information.

Membership

Dues: $50/semester

As a member you will receive:

  • A club T-shirt (in the Autumn semester)
  • Multiple weekly practice opportunities
  • Access to club equipment
  • Professional coaching
  • Ability to compete in collegiate-level competitions

Personal equipment is not a necessary component to being a member, but we do encourage that a member invests in his or her own equipment after marginal improvement and consistent membership to help reach full potential. We have awesome resources to help pick yours out when the time comes.

Because we are a self-sustaining organization, commitment is an essential element for us to thrive. It is suggested that a member attends practice once per week to see continuous growth in ability. To maintain active membership status, one must attend two meetings per academic semester.

Competitive Team

In addition to the casual aspect of the club, members are also able to compete at various indoor and outdoor tournaments held throughout the year. Examples of past tournaments our members have participated in include the Caskey Shoot at Case Western Reserve University, the Ohio State Indoor, the USA Archery National Indoor Championships, and many more.

All members, new and experienced, are encouraged to speak with one of our coaches if they are interested in competing. We will work with you to get you to where you need to be.
